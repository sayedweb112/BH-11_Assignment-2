Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Children aged 3-6 years are curious, playful, and rapidly developing skills like communication, creativity, problem-solving, and social interaction

Elementary School

Elementary school fosters foundational learning, teamwork, curiosity, and responsibility as children build skills in reading, math, science, and social development.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school years are about self-discovery, building independence, developing critical thinking, navigating friendships, and exploring academic and personal interests.
